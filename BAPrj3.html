MEMBER VALUE FOR NEW CANADIANS

This project involved conducting market research and business analysis to evaluate CAA Atlantic’s membership value proposition for new Canadians, identify service gaps, and develop strategic recommendations to improve market relevance, customer acquisition, and engagement..

Project Overview

  • Project: Member Value for New Canadians
  • Role: Business Analyst / Strategy Consultant.
  • Organization: CAA Atlantic
  • Methodology: Agile + Iterative Research Approach.
  • Project Type: Strategic Market Analysis, Product Gap Assessment & Growth Strategy
  • Duration: 8 Weeks
  • Objective: Evaluate CAA Atlantic’s existing membership offerings to determine their appeal to new Canadians, identify service gaps, and recommend strategic opportunities to improve market penetration and customer acquisition.

1. Business Problem:
CAA Atlantic sought to expand its membership base among new Canadians but lacked sufficient insights into:

  • What products and services were most relevant to newcomers
  • Existing pain points and barriers to adoption
  • How their offerings compared to competitors
  • Effective channels for reaching this target market
The organization required a data-driven business analysis to validate assumptions and support strategic decision-making

4. Requirements Gathered

Requirements were gathered through stakeholder interviews, Focus Groups, market surveys, and customer feedback sessions.
Business Requirements (BRD)

BR-001
The business shall identify and prioritize products and services that align with the unique needs, preferences, and settlement priorities of new Canadians to enhance membership relevance and value.

BR-002
The business shall evaluate existing membership offerings against competitor products and services to identify gaps, opportunities for differentiation, and areas for improvement.

BR-003
The business shall establish target customer segments based on demographic profiles, settlement stages, and service needs to support tailored product positioning and customer engagement strategies.

BR-004
The business shall define effective communication channels, messaging frameworks, and engagement strategies to improve awareness, understanding, and adoption of membership offerings among new Canadians.

BR-005
The business shall identify and evaluate strategic partnership opportunities with community organizations, settlement agencies, and related stakeholders to expand market reach and strengthen customer acquisition efforts.
